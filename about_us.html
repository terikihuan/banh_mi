About Us

Meet the founder

Mr. Nguyen founded Banh Mi Kitchen in 1997. Nguyen grew up eating banh mi everyday. According to him, one night a Banh Mi monster visit him in his dream and tell him to open a chain store. That's how he got the idea for Banh Mi Kitchen.

Our Store

Our first store was opened in HCMC, now Banh Mi Kitchen is available through out South East Asia. Mr. Nguyen is planning to expand into NA market, after Banh Mi monster has advised him to do so.
